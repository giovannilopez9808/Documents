In order to assess the air quality, we processed the measurements made with ground-based instruments in the same stations where UV Index was recorded. To recognize the individual contributions of PM10, CO, NO2, O3 were calculated the annual averages base on daily measurements from 11h to 14h LT in the period 2000-2019. The trends determined from de linear fit are: -0.1196%/year, -0.8575 %/year,  -1.04, -1.046 to PM10, CO, NO2, O3 respectively.
