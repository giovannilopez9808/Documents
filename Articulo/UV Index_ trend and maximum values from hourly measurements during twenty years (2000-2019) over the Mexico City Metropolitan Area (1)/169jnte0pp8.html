For assessing the influence of the aerosols and criteria pollutants in the UV Index, we processed the PM10, CO, NO2, O3 and AOD340 ground-based measurements carried out at the stations mentioned in Table 2. From daily measurements from 11h to 14h CST the annual mean of PM10, CO, NO2, O3 were calculated. For the case of the AOD340, the measurements along of the day were averaged. Figure \ref{610674} shows the trends of the annual values from 2000 to 2019. On the other hand, to estimate the percentage change per year (\(\epsilon\left(\%\right)\)), the slope (\(m_ {X}\)) of  the linear fit and the average in all period (\(\overline{X}_{2000-2019}\)) were used. In the case of the UVI percentage change was 0.66 % per year. In the same way, Table 3 shows the values corresponding to PM10, CO, NO2, O3 and AOD340.
