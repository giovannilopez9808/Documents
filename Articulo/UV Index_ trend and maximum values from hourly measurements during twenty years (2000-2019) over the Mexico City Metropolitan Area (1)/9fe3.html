Measurement methodology

Ground-based 

United Mexican States is in the latitudinal fringe 14.53°N - 32.71°N of the Americas, where its capital (Mexico City) lies in a basin 70 km northwest from Popocatepetl volcano, one of three highest peaks of the country with an altitude nearby 5426 m asl. The Secretariat of the Environment of the Mexico City Government (SEDEMA) of the Atmospheric Monitoring System (SIMAT) is in charge of the air quality monitoring in the Mexico City Metropolitan Area (Figure \ref{547698}).
