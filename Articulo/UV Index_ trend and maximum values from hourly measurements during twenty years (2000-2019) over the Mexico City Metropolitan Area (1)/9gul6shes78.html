The Secretariat of the Environment of the Mexico City Government by means the Atmospheric Monitoring System (SIMAT) is in charge of the air quality monitoring in the Mexico City Metropolitan Area. Since the year 2000, the biometers installed in the weather stations network of SIMAT have been measuring erythemally-weighted UV solar radiation (MED/hr). Although at the beginning only a few stations operated with these sensors, currently 11 stations are recording solar erythemal irradiance, which multiplied by a factor are converted to UV Index. The instruments Model 501A were manufactured by Solar Light Company and the calibrations were carried out every year using a reference sensor of the same factory.
