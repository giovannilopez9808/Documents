The International Commission on Illumination (CIE) established the erythemal action spectrum on the human skin using phototype II voluntarees \cite{organization2014}. According to the World Health Organization (WHO) the UVI can be calculated by the following formula:
\[I_{UV}=k_{er}\cdot\int_{250nm}^{400nm}E_{\lambda}\cdot S_{er}\left(\lambda\right)d\lambda\]
