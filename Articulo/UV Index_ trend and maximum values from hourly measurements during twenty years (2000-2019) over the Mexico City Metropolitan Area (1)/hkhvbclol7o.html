Currently, to implement a systematic method to filter the measurements with interference of clouds, are a challenge in the area. In spite of the lack of information about clouds presence during the day, the data acquired each minute in the period 2016-2018 were a great identification tool. The measurements behavior minute by minute was used to recognize the clear sky day. As expected, the monthly averages UV Index under cloudless sky were higher than \(\overline{UVI}_m\), probably due to the clouds absence.
