As was mentioned in the Introduction \ref{874460}, the phototypes III and IV are the most frequent in the country. The code in Python was modificated to compute the accumulate doses 
\(H_{er}\) as the area under the curve of the UV Index mesured along of the day. These doses were computed from UV Index non-negative integers (except zero) until reach the MED: 3.0 SED and 4.5 SED for phototypes III and IV, respectively. Figure \ref{444710} illustrates for seasons summer and winter of these determinations.
