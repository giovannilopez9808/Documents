Graphic representation of threshold erythemal dose (\(H_{er} \)) accumulated. The integrals are calculated from each integer value of the UV Index (in colors) along the hours in clear sky day on January 2, 2017 (above) and on May 27, 2018 (bottom), corresponding to phototype III and a \(H_{er}=3.0\ SED\) (a and c) and phototype IV and a \(H_{er}=4.5\ SED\) (b and d).
